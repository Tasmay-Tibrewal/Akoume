About Us

We hear you. We see you. We're here for you.

At Akoume, we understand the challenges inherent in the pursuit of mental well-being. Derived from the Greek word meaning "we listen", Akoume embodies our commitment to providing a safe and empathetic space where individuals are heard, understood, and supported.

Our Mission

Akoume's mission is to foster a compassionate community where individuals find solace, encouragement, and empathy. We believe in providing everyone with the opportunity and support necessary to lead mentally healthy and fulfilling lives.

Our Story

Founded by three college students with a shared passion for mental health, Akoume emerged from a recognition of the stigma surrounding mental health issues. With a blend of enthusiasm and determination, we embarked on a journey to address the challenges faced by our peers grappling with stress, anxiety, and feelings of isolation. In 2024, Akoume was launched with a clear vision: to establish a nurturing space for mental health support. We noticed how many of our friends, batchmates, and even ourselves were struggling-so we decided to act.

Today and Beyond

As we continue to evolve and expand our reach, we take pride in the profound impact Akoume has had. It serves as a testament that even a group of college students can effect positive change-one individual, one conversation at a time. Akoume stands as a beacon of hope and support for those navigating their mental health journey.
