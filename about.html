About Us

We hear you. We see you. We're here for you.

At Akoume, we understand that the journey to mental well-being can be filled with challenges. "Akoume" is a Greek word meaning "to listen," and that's precisely what we do. We provide a safe and compassionate space where you can feel heard, understood, and supported on your path towards improved mental health.

Our Mission

Akoume's mission is to break down the stigma surrounding mental health and make support accessible to everyone in our community. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live a mentally healthy and fulfilling life.

Our Story

Honestly, it all started with boredom. We are just a bunch of college students, while hanging out we were feeling bored, and wanting to do something more meaningful than binge-watching another series.

We all did share a passion for mental health. We'd also noticed how many of our friends, batchmates, and even ourselves were struggling with things like stress, anxiety, and feeling disconnected. That's when it hit us: why not use our time and energy to actually make a difference?

So, with a mix of excitement and the slightest bit of "are we crazy?" we launched Akoume. Yes, there were late nights, tons of brainstorming sessions, and definitely some overwhelming moments. But, through it all, we never lost sight of our goal: to create a safe and welcoming space for everyone needing mental health support.
