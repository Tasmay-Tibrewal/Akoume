About Us

We hear you. We see you. We're here for you.

At Akoume, we understand the challenges inherent in the pursuit of mental well-being. Derived from the Greek word meaning "we listen," Akoume embodies our commitment to providing a safe and empathetic space where individuals are heard, understood, and supported.

Our Mission:

Akoume's mission is to foster a compassionate community where individuals find solace, encouragement, and empathy. We believe in providing everyone with the opportunity and support necessary to lead mentally healthy and fulfilling lives.

Our Story:

Founded by three college students with a shared passion for mental health, Akoume emerged from a recognition of the stigma surrounding mental health issues. With a blend of enthusiasm and determination, we embarked on a journey to address the challenges faced by our peers grappling with stress, anxiety, and feelings of isolation. In 2024, Akoume was launched with a clear vision: to establish a nurturing space for mental health support. We all did share a passion for mental health. We'd also noticed how many of our friends, batchmates, and even ourselves were struggling with things like stress, anxiety, and feeling disconnected. That's when it hit us: why not use our time and energy to actually make a difference?

Today and Beyond:

As we continue to evolve and expand our reach, we take pride in the profound impact Akoume has had.
